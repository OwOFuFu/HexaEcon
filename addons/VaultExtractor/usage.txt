This HexaEcon extension allows you to convert your balance from Vault to HexaEcon automatically
Depends: HexaEcon, Vault

1) Download the extension
2) Put into server and make sure you have Vault and HexaEcon
3) When player join and have a vault account the extension converts automatically to hexaecon bank (check the console)
